# Clinical Content Review Checklist

Use this checklist whenever an interaction record is created or updated.

## Record Identity

- Confirm the drug pair is correct.
- Confirm the pair is stored in normalized order.
- Confirm the interaction record is not a duplicate of an existing pair.
- Confirm the record is linked to the correct severity.

## Severity Review

- Confirm the severity label is appropriate.
- Confirm the severity matches the clinical impact.
- Confirm the severity badge/class mapping is correct in the application.
- Confirm the severity priority is appropriate for display and sorting.

## Clinical Mechanism

- Confirm the mechanism statement is clinically accurate.
- Confirm the mechanism is specific to the exact drug pair.
- Confirm the mechanism does not overstate certainty.

## Clinical Effect

- Confirm the clinical effect is accurate and complete.
- Confirm the effect describes the expected patient risk.
- Confirm the effect does not claim more than the evidence supports.

## Management Recommendation

- Confirm the management recommendation is practical.
- Confirm the recommendation is consistent with current clinical guidance.
- Confirm the recommendation is clear enough for frontline use.

## Monitoring Advice

- Confirm the monitoring advice is relevant and realistic.
- Confirm the monitoring advice includes the right patient parameters.
- Confirm the monitoring advice does not omit important safety checks.

## Evidence Review

- Confirm the evidence level is appropriate.
- Confirm the evidence level is not overstated.
- Confirm the evidence level aligns with the available references.

## Reference Review

- Confirm the reference field is accurate.
- Confirm the reference is current enough for use.
- Confirm any placeholder text has been replaced where possible.

## Data Quality

- Confirm the record has no spelling or formatting errors.
- Confirm optional fields are only filled when supported.
- Confirm notes do not introduce unsupported claims.
- Confirm the record is active or inactive as intended.

## Audit Fields

- Record the date reviewed.
- Record the reviewer name.
- Record the approval status.

## Approval Status

- Approved
- Approved with edits
- Needs further review
- Rejected

## Final Safety Check

- Confirm the record remains clinically cautious.
- Confirm the application disclaimer still applies.
- Confirm the content is suitable for pharmacist review before use in patient care.
